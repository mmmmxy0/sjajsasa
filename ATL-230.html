Features

Afidus Battery Technology™

The best time lapse camera with Afidus exclusive energy saving technology, recording up to 4 months with only 4 AA batteries. (5 mins interval)

Afidus Smart Focus Technology™

Exclusive motorized zoom and focus lens, equal to DSLR 16-35mm, the one and only zoom time lapse camera with Wi-Fi APP control. Super wide angle HFOV 120°

Weather Proof

IPX5 weather proof design, time lapse anytime and anywhere. Silicone sleeve accessory provides complete weather proof against water damage.

Image Alignment

Exclusive feature in Afidus APP enables you to have the same image composition after relocating your camera or changing batteries.

Waterproof USB port

Exclusive waterproof micro-USB port, power supply continuously without any concern.

Mult i-schedule

Exclusive multi-schedule feature allows you recording timelapse video in 4 different period in a day.

Battery Pack Selector

To extend the time lapse recording hours, you can connect to your own battery pack. Afidus gives you different options to enable the best recording duration.
