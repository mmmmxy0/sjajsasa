Our Story


Afidus Technologies Inc. is a Taiwan-based company that specializes in the design, development, and production of professional-grade time-lapse cameras and related solutions. Established in 2012, Afidus has quickly grown to become a leading provider of innovative and reliable time-lapse camera systems for a wide range of industries and applications, including construction, art, science, education, and entertainment.

Afidus' mission is to empower people to capture and share the beauty of time through its state-of-the-art time-lapse camera systems. The company is committed to delivering high-quality, user-friendly, and cost-effective solutions that meet the evolving needs of its customers. To achieve this, Afidus invests heavily in research and development, and collaborates closely with industry experts and partners to stay at the forefront of technological advancements and market trends.

Afidus' products have been widely adopted by customers around the world, including major construction firms, museums, art galleries, scientific institutions, and media companies. Its cameras have been used to document the construction of iconic buildings, capture the beauty of nature, record scientific experiments, and create mesmerizing visual effects for movies and commercials.

In conclusion, Afidus Technologies Inc. is a dynamic and innovative company that has established itself as a leading provider of time-lapse camera systems. Its commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability has earned it a loyal and diverse customer base and positioned it for continued growth and success in the years to come.
