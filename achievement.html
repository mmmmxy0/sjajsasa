These cameras will document the entire 36-month construction timeline, capturing every stage of the ‘YUESION Jingxuan’ project from groundbreaking to completion. This initiative not only represents a significant demonstration project for YUESION in Xindian but also showcases the innovative application of Time Lapse technology within hazardous old building reconstruction. Smart Choice: ATL230 Takes Centre Stage The Afidus ATL230 selected by YUESION boasts multiple professional-grade features. Firstly, its intelligent, user-centric design incorporates ‘wireless, ladder-free’ operation, enabling users to monitor and adjust shooting parameters in real-time via a dedicated app, significantly reducing on-site maintenance risks. The waterproof, monocoque body design ensures long-term outdoor durability without requiring additional waterproof casings. Secondly, the ATL230 incorporates a Sony starlight-grade sensor with 3x optical zoom, supports 1080p high-definition recording, and delivers up to 210 days of continuous operation per charge. Combined with intelligent power management and mobile power solutions, it can reliably support the entire three-year construction period. Reliable Technology, Field-Tested Across Multiple Sites Afidus equipment has been extensively deployed in major projects such as the Xindian Shisizhang History Park and Taoyuan Airport's Third Runway. Its ‘image comparison anti-drift’ function automatically detects lens displacement, ensuring consistent footage. Installed at the site's highest vantage points and access points, the cameras provide comprehensive progress monitoring. Complemented by app tutorials, they enable the YUESION team to remotely configure filming schedules, schedule battery and memory card replacements, perform cloud backups, and secure optimal angles via parapet wall clamps and C-channel steel mounting. Visual Value: A New Tool for Hazardous Building Reconstruction YUESION Development has long been renowned for its ‘full-project management’ approach. The introduction of Time Lapse photography not only comprehensively documents construction details but also transforms this footage into brand marketing material, strengthening public trust in hazardous building reconstruction. For the client, Time Lapse imagery serves not merely as documentation but as a vital tool for conveying corporate professionalism and responsibility. Conclusion Technology merges with engineering, making ‘time’ the most compelling narrator. The Afidus ATL230, with its intelligent design, extended battery life, and professional-grade imaging quality, delivers world-class documentation standards for the ‘YUESION Jingxuan’ project. Over the next three years, every transformation of this hazardous old building site will be perfectly preserved through Time Lapse imagery, leaving an eternal testament to the birth of Xindian's new landmark.

Carrying out large-scale projects in prime urban locations presents not only technical construction challenges but also the professional task of consistently capturing high-quality documentation throughout the entire process. Today, Afidus, in collaboration with Yuan-Yang Construction Co., Ltd., officially introduced the Afidus ATL230 professional Time Lapse cameras at the Far Eastern Department Store Baoqing Redevelopment Project on Zhonghua Road in Taipei. Over the course of the 52-month construction timeline, the cameras will provide uninterrupted, world-class visual documentation.
Key Challenges – Clear Project Pain Points Located in the heart of Taipei, surrounded by multiple high-rise buildings and adjacent to residential areas, the project posed strict requirements from the client: “non-invasive installation” and “no reliance on city power.” With fixed rental points for equipment placement, it was also critical to ensure a consistent viewpoint over a long period of time while maintaining clear and stable imagery through typhoon seasons and rainy weather.

Afidus ATL230 Solutions
To meet these challenges, Afidus delivered a comprehensive solution:
• Extended Power Endurance: Four AA alkaline batteries can support up to seven months of operation, eliminating the need for external power supplies.
• Non-Invasive Installation: Custom gravity clamps allow mounting directly on parapet walls without drilling, preserving building integrity.
• Professional Imaging: Equipped with 3x optical zoom and remote APP control, the cameras easily focus on core construction areas, ensuring high-resolution images.
• Image Alignment Correction: AI-enabled auto-detection and correction of camera shifts guarantee consistent framing throughout multi-phase construction.

Implementation Highlights and Results
During installation, the Afidus team designed custom clamps for 20 cm and 30 cm parapet walls, enabling setup within just 5–10 minutes per unit. Through real-time APP connectivity, project stakeholders were able to confirm framing remotely, without needing to climb on-site, greatly improving efficiency and reducing labor costs. Strong Client Endorsement A local community representative praised: “The installation was surprisingly fast and professional, causing no inconvenience to nearby residents.” Yuan-Yang Construction also shared positive feedback: the APP was user-friendly and intuitive, image quality remained clear even in rainy conditions, and the AI image alignment function gave them confidence. They confirmed plans to continue using Afidus solutions for future projects.
Conclusion
This collaboration once again demonstrates the strong advantages of the Afidus ATL230 in complex urban construction environments—long-lasting endurance, flexible installation, remote operation, and stable imaging—proving to be the best solution for “non-invasive high-altitude deployment” and “world-class visual documentation.” Looking ahead, Afidus will continue to empower major construction projects with innovative technology, leaving behind complete Time Lapse records of urban transformation.
