The 64th MBC Construction EXPO

10:00~18:00, 17-20 August, 2023 Kintex-ro, IIsanseo-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do, Republic of Korea


The 64th MBC Construction EXPO

The MBC Building Expo South Korea, now in its 64th year, is the country's most influential building exhibition, bringing together products and information in the fields of architecture, building materials, country houses, construction tools, information technology, lighting, landscape design, horticulture, interior and exterior landscaping, HVAC, renewable energy, and other related fields. The exhibition was held at KINTEX, the largest exhibition hall in Seoul, with a total of 462 exhibitors showcasing the highest level of competence. Afidus participated in the 64th MBC Architecture Expo in South Korea, showcasing its latest 4K timelapse camera, the ATL-800, as well as its long-established mainstay models, the ATL-200S and ATL-201S, and an IP camera with 4G wireless transmission, providing a comprehensive solution to meet the needs of a wide range of users. Afidus timelapse cameras are suitable for all kinds of fields and can record the whole construction process, which is the best assistant to record the performance process of enterprises as well as the achievements of individuals. During the exhibition, Afidus timelapse cameras attracted the attention of many professional customers. They believe that Afidus time-lapse cameras can not only ensure that the construction process is fully recorded to ensure quality, but also generate time-lapse videos that are excellent media materials for brand image promotion. In particular, the ATL-800 with 4K ultra-high resolution received high attention, which fully demonstrates Afidus' leading position in terms of high quality and has been widely recognized by the industry. We look forward to seeing Afidus’ timelapse cameras continue to demonstrate their outstanding performance at future shows, providing quality recordings and solutions for professional needs in different fields.

Digital Construction Week 2023

The Time Lapse Store Triumphs at Digital Construction Week 2023 with Afidus Timelapse Cameras London, 7th. May The Time Lapse Store, a renowned distributor of Afidus timelapse cameras in the United Kingdom, celebrated a resounding success at the highly acclaimed Digital Construction Week 2023 exhibition. Held at the esteemed LondonExCel International Exhibition Center, the event garnered widespread praise and recognition. Digital Construction Week serves as a premier platform for showcasing the latest innovations and technologies in the construction industry. The Time Lapse Store seized the opportunity to showcase the exceptional features and performance of Afidus timelapse cameras to the construction community. The Time Lapse Store's booth captivated attendees with an impressive display of Afidus timelapse cameras, highlighting their unrivaled precision and clarity in capturing and documenting construction projects. The cameras' advanced functionalities, including remote monitoring and high-resolution image quality, were highly acclaimed by industry professionals and enthusiasts alike. The positive feedback received by The Time Lapse Store affirms the excellence of Afidus timelapse cameras as invaluable tools for monitoring and documenting construction projects.
