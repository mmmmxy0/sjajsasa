Our Story

Afidus is a Taiwan-based company renowned for its specialization in the manufacturing of timelapse cameras. Established in 2006 with a vision to revolutionize the world of photography and videography, Afidus has carved a niche for itself in the competitive market by focusing on innovative technology, superior quality, and user-friendly design. Afidus timelapse cameras are designed to cater to both amateur enthusiasts and professional users. We offer a range of products that boast advanced features such as high-resolution imaging, long battery life, robust weatherproofing, and user-friendly interfaces. Their flagship models often include features like 4K pixel, Wi-Fi connectivity, programmable shooting schedules, and extensive storage capabilities, making them ideal for long-term projects.
