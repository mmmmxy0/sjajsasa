Our Story

Afidus Technologies Inc. is a Taiwan-based company that specializes in the design, development, and production of professional-grade time-lapse cameras and related solutions. Established in 2012, Afidus has quickly grown to become a leading provider of innovative and reliable time-lapse camera systems for a wide range of industries and applications, including construction, art, science, education, and entertainment. Afidus' mission is to empower people to capture and share the beauty of time through its state-of-the-art time-lapse camera systems. The company is committed to delivering high-quality, user-friendly, and cost-effective solutions that meet the evolving needs of its customers. To achieve this, Afidus invests heavily in research and development, and collaborates closely with industry experts and partners to stay at the forefront of technological advancements and market trends.
